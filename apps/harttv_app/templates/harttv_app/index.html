Welcome to HartTV! This project, created by Michael Hartley, was born out of a love of watching television content and discussing it with friends. Registered users can favorite shows, write reviews, get information on individual episodes, and contribute to the rankings.

When you click on "shows" in the navigation bar, you'll see a list of all the shows that users have ever looked for and clicked on through our search feature. The data for these shows are retrieved using an awesome api called TVMAZE.

If you don't see a show that you've watched in this list of shows, fret not! Simply look for the show with our search bar and you'll be returned a page of the top 10 shows matching your query. Clicking on any of these shows will dynamically generate that show's page and add it to our database!

Through our community tab, you'll find our user dashboard. Displayed here will be a list of users who have joined the site. Click a user to see their favorites, top rated shows, and all the reviews they've ever left on a show. Lastly, you can leave a message on their user wall and discuss exciting television content!
